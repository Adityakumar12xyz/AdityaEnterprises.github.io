WhatsApp Magic Bridge (Automated Free Mode)

How to use (Hindi/English steps):

  • Step 1: `web.whatsapp.com` ko dusre tab mein open karein aur login karein.
  • Step 2: WhatsApp wali tab pe Right Click karke Inspect (ya Ctrl + Shift + J) dabayein.
  • Step 3: Agar "Console" nahi dikh raha, toh upar tabs mein "Console" pe click karein.
  • Step 4: Agar console mana kare, toh pehle `allow pasting` likh kar Enter dabayein, phir Magic Code paste karein.
  • Step 5: Magic Code ko Paste karein aur Enter daba dein.
  • Step 6: Wapis is app pe aayein aur Launch Broadcast pe click karein!
⚠️ Important: WhatsApp tab ko band mat karna jab tak sending khatam na ho jaye.
